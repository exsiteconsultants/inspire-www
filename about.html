Providing pathways into a career in sport

The Story so far

After taking a role at Fleet Town Girls & Ladies FC in 2017 IGAs found, Darren Moggach, identified the level of local talent, but found there was no pathway for those players to continue their journey, and ultimately, their dream to play professional football. Darren has more recently taken on the role as Chairman for this club.

After 2 years of research, in 2021 an outline design was produced. The idea was to create a community project that would open the door for all local talent and build teams to play at the highest competitive level available. In addition, pillars would be created that would support these players from a technical, physical and mental point, which would prepare them for the professional game in the future.

In November 2021, Darren was introduced to Sophie Hollis from IGF, and as they shared the same aspirations, on the back of this design, they joined forces and IGF FC was formed. Sophie would Chair the project, while Darren took up the role as Community Development Officer.

After a successful first season, in the summer of 2023 the project moved into phase 2. With this, the name was changed to Inspire Girls Academy (IGA) and a new committee was formed. Sophie would continue to Chair the Project, supported by a new Vice Chair, Treasurer, CWO and Secretary.

WHO ARE WE?

Inspire Girls Academy is a female only football club that compete in the JPL Warriors League. IGA create positive pathways for players into women's football, with a focus on player development.

We are based in Surrey/Hampshire, UK.

OUR MISSION

Inspire Girls Academy’s ultimate goal is to INSPIRE, develop and empower female players to be able to reach their highest potential, while helping create leaders and individuals, that will inspire others to do the same.

Our core values are ‘Respect, Honesty and Integrity’, and we believe that a positive attitude creates a positive environment.

OUR VISION

We are a non-profit club which aims to provide a talent pathway for elite players through our partnered community clubs, who will continue to provide the grassroots development, while we provide the essential support pillars to prepare the girls for high level football.

OUR PASSION

We are run by a female chair, which is further supported by experienced male and female coaches and managers.

Everyone involved understands the importance and core goals of our club and is here to ensure all who play under the Inspire Girls Academy FC banner do so with a sense of pride and belonging.
